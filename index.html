I'm Carlos Jiménez

Who I am

I'm a Gameplay Programmer with strong skills in C++, C#, Unity, Godot and Software architecture. I've developed and published several indie minigames and prototypes on my own as well as collaborating with artists, designers and other programmers.

I've been playing videogames almost everyday since I was able to grab a controller, which has given me a lot of experience about the mechanics and designs of lots of genres. I'm also a usual pen 'n paper player and game master, as well as a long fan of card and board games.

My background working with web technologies helped me develop my coding and interpersonal skills, collaborating with different departments and clients.

What I'm doing

Livraria João

Practice Portuguese with this cute 3D game where you manage a bookshop, selling classic brasilian books and solving exercises. Yet to be released.

  • Unity (C#).
  • Use of Unity's Navigation.
  • State Machines.
  • Multiple 2D UI minigames (word search, drag and drop, choose correct answers, etc.).
  • Avatar customization.
  • Saving and loading data.
  • Adapted to WebGL, Android and desktop.
Project Apax

Classic turn-based 3D game inspired by FFIX and Golden Sun.

  • Unity (C#).
  • Behavior trees for complex enemy and bosses AI.
  • Combat system.
  • Dialogue system.
  • Customizable load screens.
  • Scene persistance.
Spooky Hat Shop

Master's Final Degree Project. Overcooked-like game about managing a hat shop for ghosts with procedurally generated dungeons.

  • Unreal Engine 4.27 (C++).
  • Part of a team of over 40 students.
  • Procedurally generated dungeons and puzzles.
  • Multiple complex AIs.
  • 2D minigames for hat crafting and multiple purpose UI.
  • Use of Perforce version control.
